# Engineering Standards: Colloidal Stabilization and Embolism Prevention

This document defines the mechanical and chemical parameters required to encapsulate organic plant-based lipids into stable, non-coalescing micro-capsules that simulate human chylomicrons without triggering pulmonary fat embolisms.

## 1. Organic Surfactant and Matrix Phase
To prevent droplet aggregation (coalescence), the lipid phase must be wrapped in an organic-certified surfactant monolayer:
*   **Primary Surfactant:** Certified Organic **Soy or Sunflower Lecithin** (standardized to contain >60% Phosphatidylcholine).
*   **Co-Surfactant:** Organic **Yucca or Quillaja extract** (rich in natural saponins) to lower interfacial tension and stabilize the outer membrane charge.
*   **Lipid Core:** Cold-pressed organic High-Oleic Sunflower Oil or organic Flaxseed Oil (providing essential omega-3 fatty acids).

## 2. Mechanical Homogenization Protocol
Micro-capsules must match the physical clearance constraints of the human microvasculature. Any droplet exceeding 5 microns will cause mechanical occlusion in capillary beds.

### Operational Steps:
1.  **Pre-Emulsion:** Blend the organic oil core (40% v/v) with the aqueous lecithin/saponin solution (60% v/v) using a high-speed rotor-stator mixer at **8,000 RPM** for 15 minutes.
2.  **High-Pressure Microfluidization:** Pass the pre-emulsion through a high-pressure homogenizer for **5 discrete passes** at a minimum operating pressure of **15,000 PSI**.
3.  **Temperature Control:** Encapsulation is highly exothermic. The output lines must pass through an organic-compatible ice-water heat exchanger to keep the fluid temperature below **35°C**, preventing lipid peroxidation.

## 3. Quality Assurance Thresholds
*   **Particle Size Distribution (PDI):** Must be $< 0.15$ using Dynamic Light Scattering (DLS), indicating a highly uniform capsule size.
*   **Critical Diameter Max ($D_{99}$):** 100% of particles must measure under **200 nanometers**.
*   **Zeta Potential:** Must maintain a surface charge between **-35mV and -50mV** to generate the electrostatic repulsion necessary to prevent clotting when mixed with the *Apis* plasma phase.
